About Us

TSS namely “Triranga Seva Sangha”, is one of India’s largest independent and self-volunteer non-profit organizations. TSS was founded on 26 January 2004 by a small group of friends with a strong passion to change society today and build a better India for tomorrow. The organization provides youngsters a platform to serve the society and bridges the gap between the educated and the uneducated. It also brings the problems of the helpless and the homeless and the underprivileged to the forefront of the public eye and contacts government, private institutions, and individuals for their immediate solution and resolution. Creating awareness to donate blood, planting lots of trees are major steps taken every year by our organization since formation.
